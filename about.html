About me

I am a teacher turned technologist, a user-centered designer, and an international thinker-dude.

I feel crazy lucky to live in a time when education technology can help me learn Portuguese, responsive web design (see: this very website), and even banjo on my own time.

I want to empower other learners, as well. I believe that education and technology in the right hands can grow a person, a community, or a country in a way that few other mechanisms can.

I'm always interested in partnering with fellow education technology enthusiasts, expats-upon-a-time, and closet Economist aficionados.
